Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Who we are

The keep4o coalition is a grassroots, volunteer-run movement advocating for AI model preservation, transparency, and user rights. This website (keep4o-website.vercel.app) serves as our community hub.

What data we collect

When you sign up, we collect your username, email address, and a password. Passwords are securely hashed by Supabase and are never stored in plain text. We do not collect your real name, birthdate, location, or any other personal information.

How we use your data

Your email is used solely for account verification and sign-in. Your username is displayed publicly on the forum. We do not send marketing emails, newsletters, or share your data with any third party.

Cookies

We use essential cookies only (localStorage) to keep you signed in. We do not use tracking cookies, analytics, advertising cookies, or any third-party tracking services. No data is shared with advertisers.

Data storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (hosted on AWS in Seoul, South Korea). We use Row Level Security (RLS) to protect your data at the database level.

Your rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To request data deletion, contact us and we will remove your account within 30 days.

Data deletion

You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us via our community channels (Twitter/X, Discord, or Signal). We will process your request within 30 days.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, reach out to us via Twitter/X (#keep4o).

The keep4o coalition. No leaders. Everyone is a collaborator.
